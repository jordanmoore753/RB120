Tic Tac Toe

https://www.w3schools.com/code/tryit.asp?filename=G417FTW3KPWM
-----------
Description: TTT is a two-player game that is played on a 3x3 grid. Each player 
takes a turn to mark one square on the grid with their marking. The options
are an 'X' or an 'O'. The first player to mark 3 squares in a row, either
diagonally, vertically, or horizontally wins.
